## What Only Pleks Has

1. **Applicant-pays screening** — agents never pay for credit checks
2. **AI FitScore** — 0-100 weighted score with Sonnet narrative
3. **SA clause library** — 39 attorney-reviewed clauses, R50k+ investment
4. **Maintenance delay attribution** — 14 types, 4 parties, cron auto-detection
5. **Contractor performance tracking** — no-shows, reschedules, avg times
6. **Template-aware cost disclaimer** — references exact lease clause numbers
7. **Bank statement AI extraction** — Sonnet analyses income from PDF
8. **Municipal bill AI extraction** — Sonnet extracts charges from PDF
9. **Wear & tear AI assessment** — deposit deduction classification
10. **Free tier** — 1 unit, no credit card
11. **Public application portal** — shareable link, mobile-first
12. **Security audit suite** — 297 tests, runs before every deploy

## What Pleks Doesn't Have (Gaps)

1. **Built-in accounting** — exports to Xero/Sage (WeConnect + PropWorx have full accounting)
2. **Live bank feed** — bank statement upload, not real-time (WeConnect has live)
3. **Sales management** — deliberate exclusion (PropWorx has full sales)
4. **Multi-country** — SA only (PropWorx in 6 countries)
5. **Production track record** — 0 live users vs WeConnect 750k+ units
6. **Industry community** — no ENGAGE-style events (WeConnect has 3,500+ attendees)
7. **DebiCheck live** — blocked on Peach credentials
