Why Pleks

Two things the others don't do. We built the product around them.

Every other claim on this page — the automation, the statements, the DebiCheck run — falls out of getting these two right. Everything else is table stakes.

01 SECURITY · POSTURE

Every action keeps a receipt. Your clients' PII never leaves the vault unmasked.

The audit_log table is append-only — no UPDATE, no DELETE, ever. Identity numbers, bank details and addresses are encrypted at rest, decrypted only when sent to Searchworx, and masked for every UI render. Each credit check carries a signed consent_token — no consent, no check, no exceptions.

  • AImmutable audit log. 7-year retention, Merkle-hashed, exportable as a signed Tribunal bundle.
  • BPII encrypted at rest. AES-256, KMS-rotated. Masked by default in every screen; viewer-ID and reason logged on every unmask.
  • CConsent-gated credit checks. POPIA §11 consent_token required — check is refused if it's missing, expired, or withdrawn.
  • DRow-level segregation. org_id on every table, enforced at the database. No cross-agency leakage is possible, not just unlikely.
  • ESA-resident, SA-owned. Supabase JHB region. No sub-processor outside the country without explicit opt-in.
02 SCREENING · ECONOMICS

Applicants pay. You get a FitScore, not a 40-page credit report.

Applicants submit details, consent, and pay R399 directly. Pleks runs the Searchworx bundle — credit, ID, deeds — and hands back a single decision-ready number with the rationale attached. You never pay for a check that goes nowhere. You never read a raw credit report you aren't trained to interpret.

  • AApplicant-paid, R399. PayFast redirect, receipt emailed. Joint applications billed once (R749).
  • BFitScore 0–100. Weighted: credit (40) · income affordability (30) · tenancy history (20) · ID/deeds integrity (10). Sonnet-drafted rationale, practitioner-reviewed.
  • CEvery applicant shown. Low scores don't get filtered out — legal protection for you, transparency for them.
  • D30% affordability rule. Flagged explicitly. No guessing what "marginal" means.
  • EShortlist → sign. Accept an applicant and the DebiCheck mandate, lease draft, and move-in inspection slot are queued automatically.

I did your job for eleven years. Then I built the software I wished existed.
